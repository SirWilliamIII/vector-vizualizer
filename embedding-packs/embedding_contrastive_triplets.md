# Contrastive Triplets (Anchor / Positive / Negative)

Use these for evaluating similarity structure.

## Triplet 1
Anchor: Restart the computer.
Positive: Reboot the machine.
Negative: Start the dishwasher.

## Triplet 2
Anchor: Schedule a meeting for tomorrow.
Positive: Book an appointment for the next day.
Negative: Order a meal for pickup.

## Triplet 3
Anchor: How do I bake sourdough bread?
Positive: Tips for making homemade bread.
Negative: How to install Docker.

## Triplet 4

Negative: Summarize the history of France.
