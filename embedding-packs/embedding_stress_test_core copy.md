# Embedding Stress-Test Set (Core)

## 1. Near-Duplicates
1. Book me a table for two at 7.
2. Reserve dinner for two at 7pm.
3. I need a restaurant reservation this evening.
4. Can you grab us a dinner spot?

## 2. Opposites With Similar Surface Form
5. Increase the brightness.
6. Decrease the brightness.
7. Turn the lights up.
8. Turn the lights down.

## 3. Polysemy (Ambiguous Words)
9. The bank closed early today.
10. We set up camp on the left bank of the river.
11. I need to deposit this check at the bank.

## 4. Long vs Short
12. Restart.
13. Please reboot the device by powering it off completely, waiting ten seconds, and turning it back on again.

## 5. Noisy Queries
14. plz restar teh ph0ne
15. rstart the fon pls
16. Restart the phone.

## 6. Cross-Domain Divergence
17. How do I cook basmati rice?
18. How do I configure NGINX?
19. How do I fix a flat tire?
20. How do I renew my passport?

## 7. Multilingual Equivalents
21. Where is the closest train station?
22. ¿Dónde está la estación de tren más cercana?
23. Où se trouve la gare la plus proche ?
24. Wo ist der nächste Bahnhof?

## 8. High vs Low Context
25. Load the dataset from S3 and vectorize the text fields.
26. Load the groceries from the car.
